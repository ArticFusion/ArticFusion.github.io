What to do?

Reykjavik is the capital city of Iceland and has captivating areas to explore around the city such as walking around the market and exploring Icelandic designs in small boutiques. The streets are named after Nordic religious figures such as Odin, Thor, Loki and Freya. The Reykjavik nightlife is famous for live entertainment, stand up, comedy theatre and opera.
